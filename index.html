Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the workers verified?

Every helper undergoes a multi-step verification process including citizenship ID verification, a face-to-face interview with our team, and reference checks from previous employers in the Butwal area.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

Usually, we recommend customers provide basic supplies (vacuum, mop, cleaning liquids) to ensure the hygiene standards you prefer. However, we can arrange for the worker to bring supplies for an additional fee if requested.

What if I am not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are unhappy with a service, contact us within 24 hours. We will investigate the issue and, if necessary, provide a re-clean or a credit for your next booking.

Can I book the same person every time?

Yes! Many of our customers prefer consistency. Once you find a helper you love, you can request them for all future bookings, subject to their availability.

How do I pay for the service?

You pay the worker directly after the job is completed. We currently support Cash and eSewa payments. The price agreed upon during the booking is what you pay—no hidden fees.

Is SewaKhoj available outside Butwal?

Currently, we focus exclusively on Butwal and immediate surrounding areas like Tilottama and Bhairahawa to ensure we maintain the highest quality of service and quick response times.
